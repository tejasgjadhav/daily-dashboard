Indian markets closed Thursday's session sharply higher for the second consecutive day, with the Nifty 50 gaining 277 pts (+1.18%) to 23,689.60 and the Sensex surging 789.74 pts (+1.06%) to 75,398.72. India VIX cooled to ~18.44, a tentative positive signal, but remains above comfortable levels. IT stocks continue to face selling pressure despite the Nasdaq rally — a domestic theme around AI disruption. Tata Steel Q4 results are due today and will set the metals sector tone.
